Alexander Broom

Founder

Alexander Broom is the founder of Mouse and Trigger. In order to finish Mouse and Trigger, Alex forbid himself from playing video games. Devestating! Besides working on Mouse and Trigger and playing games and going to work, Alex is in the very slow process of writing a light-hearted, fun fantasy book.

Alex's favorite video game genre is strategy games, especially ones with a human versus human element. His two favorite games are Dota 2 and Dark Souls. Currently, Alex is the dungeon master for Mouse And Trigger's Dungeon and Dragons campaign. Finally, Alex recommends that you all read Brandon Sanderson's Stormlight Archive.
