About

Welcome to Mouse And Trigger and thanks for visiting. Mouse And Trigger started so we could have our own place to share our reviews on all things. We usually write about video games, hence the name, but will review anything which peaks our interests. The various article categories will grow. In the near future, we plan on at least including Board Games, Movies, Television, Music, and Books.

Mouse and Trigger was built from the ground up in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by Alexander Broom. Everything was hand coded, and we have plans to upgrade as well. The upgrades may be slow, since development only proceeds when Alex has free time. First (and hopefully really soon), we will make all of our pages responsive. Next, we are in the process of developing a Ruby on Rails web application where our readers can create accounts and comment on everything we do. Which might be a dire mistake...

Finally, If you want to write an article or give us feedback? Contact us!

Donate

Mouse and Trigger runs completely ad-free and we want to keep it that way if possible. The entire site is run out of pocket. Therefore, we love donations and are going to blatently advertise our donate link!
